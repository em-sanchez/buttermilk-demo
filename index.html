Our story

Made by hand,
with heart.

Jamie and Kathy Otto opened Buttermilk with a simple idea — make really good food, from scratch, every day. Jamie even built the interior by hand, giving the space that warm, lived-in feel the moment you walk in.

Everything on the menu is handcrafted daily. The biscuits are legendary. The cinnamon rolls are the size of your head. And the coffee comes from Sarasota's own Perq Roasters.

We're tucked away on Palmer Blvd, open Tuesday through Saturday mornings. Come early — things sell out.
